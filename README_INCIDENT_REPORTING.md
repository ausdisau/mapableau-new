# Incident reporting

Participant incident reporting lives in the **Safety & incident management centre** (`/dashboard/safety`).

## Quick links

- Report a concern: `/dashboard/safety/incidents/new`
- Your reports: `/dashboard/safety/incidents`
- Support tickets: `/dashboard/safety/support`
- Full route map: [docs/safety.md](docs/safety.md)
- Phase 4 overview: [README_MAPABLE_CORE_PHASE_4.md](README_MAPABLE_CORE_PHASE_4.md)

## Behaviour

- Participants are linked as both `reportedById` and `participantId` on create.
- Submitting moves draft reports to `submitted` or `triage` when severity or safeguarding flags require it.
- Possible reportable incidents create review warnings — not automatic NDIS Commission submission.
